IOS APP FOOTER

The iOS App Footer is located on the highly trafficked homepage of our Expedia & Portfolio Brands apps. This placement offers you exposure to our EG app users, the most loyal and engaged of our brands’ customers. Enable customers to discover relevant and contextual deals and offers throughout their booking journey through a display ad on the app home screen.

Please provide the following:

Hero Image

  • Recommended dimensions: 2640px x 1485px at 96dpi.
  • Static images only.
  • Final output size: 300x250 (max. 40kb)

Logo

  • Full color or black; high resolution or vectored art.
  • File types (in order of preference): .ai, .eps, .psd, .tif or .png

    • or

  • Product, mascot, character or icon.
  • Full color; high resolution or vectored art.
  • File types (in order of preference): .ai, .eps, .psd, .tif or .png

Copy

  • Concise and catchy copy related to theme of campaign.
  • Recommended less than 12 words.
 

Dos

  • Do use photography with vibrant colors and good contrast
  • Give travelers a sense of what it’s like to be “there”, experiencing the destination/activity/landmark
  • People may be present, but they must be a part of the landscape and not the focus
  • Do use images that are high resolution and free of pixelation and blurriness
  • Do use images that leverage natural light whenever appropriate
  • Do reflect inclusion and diversity through messaging and content. Link here to best practices and examples
  • Do include Terms apply on banners when there is a *savings offer (Save up to xx%). For offers such as “free first checked bag” or something similar, if it truly is offered on all purchases, and not on specific flights (or rooms), then it is not necessary.

Don’ts

  • Images with people, animals and food as the primary subject
  • Images with over-saturation, washed-out, heavy shadows, aggressive filters (dark, motion, blur) or any Instagram-style filter treatments
  • Images that include text, are low resolution, blurry or pixelated
  • Fish-eye or distorted image lenses or treatments, including lens flare
  • Solid colors, texture backgrounds, illustrations, graphics or brand-icon centric images

Note:

  • 3rd party tags are not supported however 1x1 click trackers are accepted. 1x1 impression trackers are not allowed.
  • Ad must link in to Expedia. When clicked, landing page will open in a separate webpage in the app.
  • Expedia creative production takes approximately 5-7 business days upon receiving assets. Further requests will require additional time to implement. Please note this excludes translation or copywriting so please reach out to your AM for further timings if required.
