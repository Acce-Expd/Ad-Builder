URL SPECS

YOU ARE AN AIR/HOTEL/ACTIVITY/CAR RENTAL SUPPLIER

Ad Builder campaigns only accept LINK IN URL as landing page. In order to find the right URL for your campaign, you will have to look for your property/activity/car rental info page on the point of sale you have selected for your campaign.

Example:

You are a hotel partner based in Toronto and are called Hotel 111.
You have booked a campaign on Expedia Canada.

In order to find the right URL for your campaign, go to www.expedia.ca
Once on the Expedia Canada website, look up "Hotel 111 Toronto" and look for your property listing.
Click on your property listing, and once you land on the property page, save the web URL to use it for your campaign.
You are ready to go!

Please reach out to us at adbuilder@expediagroup.com if you have any doubts and/or questions.

 

YOU ARE A NON-ENDEMIC SUPPLIER

We may accept a link off URL to your website on a case-by-case basis.

Please reach out to us at adbuilder@expediagroup.com if you have any doubts and/or questions.
